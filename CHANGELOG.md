# Changelog - Navbar & Performance Enhancements

## [2026-03-24]

### Enhanced Sidebar (Navbar)
- **Visual Appeal**: Increased sidebar width to `w-28` for a more substantial feel.
- **Glassmorphism**: Improved background with `bg-slate-950/80` and `backdrop-blur-3xl`, plus a subtle right-side shadow.
- **Active State**: Refined the active indicator with a gradient background (`from-blue-600 to-blue-400`) and a soft blue glow.
- **Hover Effects**: Added subtle scaling (`scale-110`) and rotation (`rotate-6`) to icons on hover.
- **Profile Section**: Added a gradient border and a status indicator (online) to the user profile avatar.
- **Tooltips**: Enhanced tooltips with a blue accent border and smoother "slam-in" animation.
- **Spacing**: Increased vertical spacing between navigation items for better readability and a more professional layout.

### [2026-03-24] - Navbar & Market Intelligence Refinements

#### Sidebar & Navigation
- **Scrollable Sidebar**: Implemented a scrollable navigation container within the sidebar, ensuring all icons are accessible on smaller screens.
- **Sticky Header & Footer**: The logo and profile sections are now sticky, remaining visible while the navigation items scroll.
- **Fixed Tooltip System**: Replaced absolute tooltips with a fixed-position overlay system. This prevents tooltips from being clipped by the sidebar's scroll boundaries and ensures they are always visible above the main content.
- **Refined Spacing**: Adjusted vertical gaps and padding for a more balanced and professional layout.

#### Market Intelligence
- **Simplified UI**: Removed the Fear & Greed barometer from the Market Intelligence tab to streamline the presentation and focus on core data.
- **War & Geopolitical News**: Added a new section for "War & Geopolitical Risk" with real-time AI-driven news cards, providing critical context for market volatility.
- **Economic Calendar Filter**: Enhanced the date filtering system with a dedicated calendar date picker and improved layout for better usability.

#### Core Functionality & Responsiveness
- **Cross-Device Verification**: Verified and ensured that "New Account", "New Trade", and "New Strategy" functions work seamlessly across all screen sizes (Mobile, Tablet, Desktop).
- **Mobile UI Refinements**: Adjusted form layouts and grid systems to maintain high usability on smaller screens.
